Local Heavy Haul Trucking Rooted in Southern Utah

We're not a national carrier dropping a truck in your area. We're here. We know these roads, these towns, and what it takes to get the job done in Washington County.

Built for the People Who Build Southern Utah

St. George Heavy Haul was built around a simple idea: contractors, ranchers, and construction crews in Southern Utah deserve a heavy haul trucking company that actually shows up, does the job right, and doesn't make you chase them down for answers.

We operate out of St. George, Utah — not some distant dispatch center. When you call us, you're talking to someone who knows the roads in Washington County, understands local permit requirements, and has driven past your job site more than once.

What We Do

We specialize in heavy haul trucking in St. George, Utah and throughout Washington County. That means flatbed transport, oversized and permitted loads, equipment hauling, and construction material delivery. If it's heavy, wide, or oversized — we move it.

Our equipment includes flatbed, step-deck, and lowboy trailers configured to handle everything from a single excavator to a full flatbed load of masonry block for your job site. We'll match the right equipment to your load — not just whatever's available.

The Way We Work

We don't believe in making the quoting process complicated. Tell us what you're moving, where it starts, and where it needs to go. We'll give you a price. If you want to move forward, we'll schedule it and show up on time.

When it comes to oversized load transport, we handle the permit work with the Utah Division of Motor Vehicles and any county or municipal jurisdictions involved. You shouldn't have to navigate government paperwork on top of running your operation.

We serve the entire St. George metro area, including Washington City, Hurricane, Ivins, Santa Clara, and Cedar City to the north. We also run loads into Mesquite, Nevada regularly. If your project is nearby and you're not sure if we cover your area, just call — chances are we do.

Who We Work With

Our customers include general contractors, electrical and mechanical subcontractors, excavation companies, masonry suppliers, ranchers and agricultural operations, industrial facilities, and individuals who need to move a machine or a load that won't fit in a pickup.

We don't care if you're a one-man operation or a multi-crew GC — you'll get the same service either way. The load gets picked up. The load gets delivered. That's the deal.

Licensed & Fully Insured

Every load is covered. We carry all required commercial carrier insurance and DOT certifications for the loads we haul.
